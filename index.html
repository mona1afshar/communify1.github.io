What is Communify?

Communify is a website intended to help citizens of a city connect to their communities in an easier manner.

Residents of a city will be able to log in by entering their city number, and will be able to ask any questions /> about their city. The IT department of your city will have entered in information necessary to answer said questions. /> For instance, if someone wanted to report a pothole on a street, they could simply ask Communify who they should call to report /> and Communify will tell you.
